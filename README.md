## Description

In a world of endless distractions, Capy's Journey helps people of all ages reclaim focus and autonomy over their minds. Capy's Journey is a fun mobile app that combines expert-informed guided meditations, daily wellness exercises, and engaging gamification to make mindfulness an experience worth returning toâ€”cultivating lasting focus, resilience, and emotional well-being.

## Features

- **Guided Meditations/Lessons**: Expert-informed bite-sized meditation sessions covering various topics in mindfulness and wellness
- **Age-Specific Content**: Tailored meditations and exercises designed for both children and adults, meeting the unique needs of each age group
- **Mood Log**: User-friendly daily mood tracking for emotional insight
- **Gratitude Log**: Record reflections and gratitudes for the day, and grow the gratitude tree
- **Dojo**: Customizable meditations to fit user preferences and goals
- **Gamification Elements**: Progress tracking, achievements, streaks, and rewards to keep users motivated
- **Charming Visual Design**: Calming aesthetics featuring adorable capybara character
